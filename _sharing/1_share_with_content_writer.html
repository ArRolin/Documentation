--- title: Share with Content Writers ---

The Content Writer share level is for sharing a site with users who are non-technical. They do not see the Code Editor or File Browser interfaces. When they edit a site it takes them straight to the Visual Editor.

To share with a site to Content Writer:

  • Go to the Sharing tab in Site Settings
  • Type in the user's email address
  • Click "Share with Developer"

Then update the share level to "View and Update Contents" and press save.
