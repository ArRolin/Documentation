Welcome to CloudCannon! The easiest way to make static and Jekyll websites updatable for clients, teams and content writers.

This Getting Started tutorial will show you in 5 minutes how to go from an HTML website on your computer to a live website your team can update on CloudCannon.

First, make sure you have:

  • A free CloudCannon account
  • An HTML website/template on your computer (if you don't have one download one of ours)
