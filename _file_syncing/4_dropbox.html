--- title: Dropbox ---

Go to your website configuration and go to storage providers and click Connect next to Dropbox.

This will bring up a dialog asking what the main source is for the initial synchronization. This only matters if you have existing files in CloudCannon or Dropbox, select the source of files you want to keep. Click Connect Dropbox.

This will take you to Dropbox, type in your credentials to continue.

Next you need to click allow to give CloudCannon access to a folder in your Dropbox.

Your Dropbox is now connected to CloudCannon.

Put files in the folder Dropbox->Apps->Cloud Cannon->Your Site Name and they will be synced to CloudCannon. Any changes made on CloudCannon will be synced back to your Dropbox.
